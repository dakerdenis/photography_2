ABOUT ME

I know you're here for a purpose – because you believe (as I do) that photography has value. For me, professional photography is a serious job, a complex technical process, which I turn into a pleasant pastime for you. I am always ready to cooperate, I am ready to listen to all your wishes and implement them as much as possible.
